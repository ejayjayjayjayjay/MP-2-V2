Menudo

Pork Menudo is the number one on the list of my comfort foods. It is a delicious dish that makes use pork, liver, and vegetables such as carrot and potato.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

1 hour.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Pork Lunch or Dinner n/a food

instructions

step 1

Combine pork, soysauce, and lemon in a bowl. Marinate for at least 1 hour.

step 2

Heat oil in a pan.

step 3

Saute garlic and onion.

step 4

Add the marinated pork. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes.

step 5

Pour in tomato sauce and water and then add the bay leaves.Let boil and simmer for 30 minutes to an hour depending on the toughness of the pork. Note: Add water as necessary.

step 6

Add-in the liver and hot dogs.Cook for 5 minutes.

step 7

Put-in potatoes, carrots, sugar,salt, and pepper. Stir and cook for 8 to 12 minutes. Serve. Share and enjoy!

ingredients inside the Meal Box

2 lbs. pork

1/4 lb. pig liver

1 cup potatoes diced

1 piece carrot cubed

1/2 cup soy sauce

1/2 piece lemon

1 piece onion chopped

3 cloves garlic minced/p>

1 teaspoon sugar

3/4 cup tomato sauce

1 cup water

4 pieces hotdogs sliced diagonally

2 tablespoons cooking oil

2 to 3 pieces dried bay leaves. and salt
